Page Title: sustainable fashion blog

A tax exemption meant for travelers has opened a loophole, allowing low-cost fashion to flood Canada and create an unfair playing field for local brands. Ultra-fast fashion companies exploit the de minimis rule, leading to economic and environmental implications for Canadian businesses and public resources. There's growing momentum to address these issues through policy adjustments in Canada while promoting responsible consumer choices.
