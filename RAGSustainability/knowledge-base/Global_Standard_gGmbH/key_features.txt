Page Title: key features

The Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS) is the worldwide leading textile processing standard for organic fibres, including environmental, human rights, and social criteria, backed up by independent third-party certification of the entire textile supply chain. GOTS certification builds upon distinctive and unique key features, including compliance support, organic fibres, due diligence, third-party certification, human rights and social criteria, environmental criteria, all processing stages, and traceability.
