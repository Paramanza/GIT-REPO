Page Title: Sponsor Memo

To require fashion retailers to map their supply chains and perform sufficient due diligence. This includes identifying, preventing, mitigating, accounting for, and taking remedial action to address actual and potential adverse impacts to human rights and the environment in their own operations and in their supply chain.
