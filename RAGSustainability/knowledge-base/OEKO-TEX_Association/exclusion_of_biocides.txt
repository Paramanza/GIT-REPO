Page Title: exclusion of biocides

OEKO-TEX® banned the use of all biologically active products due to their potentially negative influence on human health. Biocides are now forbidden in all OEKO-TEX® STANDARD 100, ORGANIC COTTON and LEATHER STANDARD certified articles for babies as well as in any other articles with direct contact to mucous membranes.
