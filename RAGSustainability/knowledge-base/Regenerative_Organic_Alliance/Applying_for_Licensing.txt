Page Title: Applying for Licensing

Applicants must submit a Brand Regenerative Organic System Plan (ROSP) for products intended to carry a Regenerative Organic Certified claim. The ROA staff will use the ROSP to prepare for desk audits and conduct traceability audits. Each actor in a Licensed business supply chain must complete the SCA Disclosure Form, which will be uploaded in the online application.
