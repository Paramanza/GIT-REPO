Page Title: MY Voice Project - SAI

MY Voice seeks to combat forced and child labor in the palm oil and garment sectors in Malaysia by increasing worker voice in social compliance systems and helping to improve communication and coordination between local actors. Project objectives include increasing the ability of workers and civil society to advocate for change, increasing worker voice, and increasing access to remedies for workers exploited for forced labor and/or child labor.
