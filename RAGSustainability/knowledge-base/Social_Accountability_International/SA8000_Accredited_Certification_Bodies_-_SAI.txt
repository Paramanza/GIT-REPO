Page Title: SA8000 Accredited Certification Bodies - SAI

Use this page to explore the list of Certification Bodies (CBs) accredited by SAAS to conduct SA8000 audits and deliver certifications. Accredited bodies provide independent oversight to ensure social audits are undertaken impartially and effectively. Organizations interested in becoming SA8000-certified should contact these accredited CBs directly.
