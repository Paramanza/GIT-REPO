Page Title: SA8000 Standard Revision - SAI

SAI is undertaking a full revision of the SA8000 Standard for Decent Work. The purpose of this revision is to ensure that the SA8000 Standard continues to lead as a benchmark for ethical workplaces, reflecting evolving human rights risks and good practices. Stakeholders are encouraged to engage in the process, provide feedback on the working draft, and participate in workshops to shape the future of the Standard.
